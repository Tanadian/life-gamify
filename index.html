Point Allocation Guidelines

×

Physical

  • 1 point: Light activity (10-15 min walk, stretching, taking stairs)
  • 2 points: Moderate activity (20-30 min walk, light workout, active chores)
  • 3 points: Good workout (45+ min exercise, gym session, sports game)
  • 4 points: Intense workout (1+ hour intense exercise, challenging hike, competitive sports)
  • 5 points: Exceptional effort (marathon training, intense 2+ hour session, major physical challenge)

Mental

  • 1 point: Light learning (reading article, podcast, quick research)
  • 2 points: Focused study (30+ min reading, online course lesson, problem-solving)
  • 3 points: Deep work (1+ hour focused learning, completing course module, skill practice)
  • 4 points: Intensive mental effort (2+ hours study, complex project work, difficult skill mastery)
  • 5 points: Major mental achievement (completing course, mastering difficult concept, significant breakthrough)

Social

  • 1 point: Brief interaction (text conversation, quick call, small talk)
  • 2 points: Meaningful conversation (30+ min chat, helping someone, group interaction)
  • 3 points: Quality social time (dinner with friends, family activity, networking event)
  • 4 points: Significant social effort (hosting event, deep conversation, community involvement)
  • 5 points: Major social milestone (public speaking, leading event, meaningful relationship building)

Creative

  • 1 point: Small creative act (doodling, quick photo, brainstorming)
  • 2 points: Creative practice (30+ min drawing/writing, trying new recipe, decorating)
  • 3 points: Focused creative work (1+ hour project, completing piece, learning technique)
  • 4 points: Substantial creative effort (major project progress, performance, complex creation)
  • 5 points: Creative breakthrough (finishing major work, public display, mastering new medium)

Productive

  • 1 point: Small task completion (organizing desk, quick admin, simple planning)
  • 2 points: Moderate productivity (completing to-do items, 1-2 hour work session, planning)
  • 3 points: Good productivity day (major task completion, 3+ focused hours, system improvement)
  • 4 points: High productivity (multiple major tasks, full productive day, significant progress)
  • 5 points: Exceptional productivity (major milestone, system overhaul, extraordinary output)
